Requirements

What qualifies

Your submission must meet these minimums to enter judging. Quality is what wins — the checklist is the floor, not the ceiling.

📝 Blog post
  • 1,200+ words, original and net-new
  • ActiveCampaign and Active Intelligence mentioned by name
  • Your affiliate link included
  • At least one screenshot or visual
  • Affiliate disclosure included
  • Published on your own site or LinkedIn
🎥 YouTube video
  • Five minutes or longer, original content
  • ActiveCampaign and Active Intelligence mentioned
  • Affiliate link in video description
  • Platform demo or screen recording included
  • Affiliate disclosure in description
  • Published on your own YouTube channel
🚫 What will not qualify
  • AI-generated content without meaningful human editing
  • Plagiarized or repurposed existing content
  • Content that does not mention ActiveCampaign
  • Pure promotional content with no educational value
  • Content published outside the contest window
What the best submissions have in common
  • They teach the reader something genuinely useful about autonomous marketing or the competitive landscape
  • They use real proof points, screenshots, and customer examples — not just feature descriptions
  • They are written in the author's own voice with a clear, original perspective
  • They include a natural, well-placed affiliate link — not forced in at the end
